This is a representation of the 16 divination signatures in the Ifá tradition of the Yorùbá people in West Africa. The signatures are named according to the binary pattern produced by combining the row and column patterns as (rrcc).

We represent the "open" or "positive" position with the number 0, and the "closed" or "negative" position with the number 1. For example, for a row name of 11 and a column name of 00, the signature produced is 1100, which is known as Irosun.

00 01 10 11
00 Ogbè (1) Ọ̀sá (10) Òtúrá (12) Ọ̀wọ́nrín (8)
01 Ìrẹtẹ̀ (11) Ọ̀fún (16) Òdí (4) Ọ̀kànràn (6)
10 Ògúndá (9) Ìwòrì (3) Ọ̀sẹ́ (15) Òtúrúpọ̀n (13)
11 Ìrosùn (7) Ìká (14) Ọ̀bàrà (5) Ọ̀yẹ̀kú (2)
